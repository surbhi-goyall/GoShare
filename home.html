About us



We believe in the power of community, sustainability, and convenience. That's why we've created this carpooling app with students, staff, and faculty in mind. Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion, lower emissions, and make your daily commute easier than ever. What sets GoShare apart is our commitment to foster a sense of community among our university members. We believe that by carpooling, you not only save time and money but also create new connections and friendships within the campus community. Together, we are making a positive impact on the environment by reducing our carbon footprint, one shared ride at a time.



Download Carpool


To download GoShare's mobile app and simplify your journey to sustainable and cost-effective commuting, follow these simple steps. If you're using an Android device, visit the Google Play Store, or for iOS users, head to the Apple App Store. In the search bar, type 'Your Carpooling Service' and look for our distinctive logo.With our app, you can conveniently find rides, connect with fellow commuters, and coordinate your shared journeys from the palm of your hand, making your daily commute smoother and more eco-friendly. Download the app today and take the first step towards a greener and more cost-efficient way to get around!
